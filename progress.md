## Current Controls

- `A/D` or left/right arrows: steer and flip.
- `W` or up arrow: move faster.
- `S` or down arrow: brake.
- `Space`: hold to boost while energy is available.
- `P/Esc`: pause.
- `R`: restart.

## Current Gameplay Features

- Downhill snowboard physics with Rigidbody2D, gravity, SurfaceEffector2D, and snow friction.
- Score from speed, tricks, powerups, and finish bonus.
- Combo multiplier for consecutive scoring actions.
- Runtime HUD for score, speed, lives, combo, multiplier, and messages.
- Powerups, finish line, camera follow, and snow weather.
- Scene music changes automatically between Menu, gameplay, and EndGame.
- Menu scene with Start, Options, Quit.
- Endgame scene with result, Retry, Main Menu, Quit.
- Three playable levels in Build Settings: `Level1`, `Level2`, and `Level3`.
